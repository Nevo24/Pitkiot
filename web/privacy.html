Privacy Policy for Pitkiot

Last updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Pitkiot ("we", "our", or "the app") is a party game application that allows players to collect and play with character notes. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Character Notes: Text content of character names you add to the game
  • Player Names: Optional names or nicknames you provide when submitting notes online

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: A unique device identifier is generated for online note collection sessions
  • Session Data: Temporary session codes for online note collection

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Enable the core game functionality (storing and displaying character notes)
  • Facilitate online note collection between players
  • Maintain game sessions and synchronize notes in real-time

4. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Character notes and game settings are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: When using the online collection feature, submitted notes are temporarily stored in Google Firebase Firestore to enable real-time synchronization. This data is deleted when you close the collection session.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For real-time note collection and synchronization. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

6. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required for the online note collection feature to synchronize notes between devices in real-time.
  • Storage: Used to save game notes and settings locally on your device.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared as necessary to provide the app's functionality:

  • Notes submitted during online collection are shared with the game host who initiated the collection session
  • Player names (if provided) are visible to the game host during note collection

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Notes stored on your device remain until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Online Collection Data: Temporarily stored during active collection sessions and deleted when the host closes the session

9. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed as a family-friendly party game and does not require personal information to function.

10. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your data by clearing the app's data in your device settings or uninstalling the app
  • Use the app offline without sharing any data
  • Choose not to provide a name when submitting notes online

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
